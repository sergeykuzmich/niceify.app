Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

1. No Data Collection

Niceify does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. We do not track your usage, behavior, or interactions with the application.

2. No Analytics or Tracking

The application does not include any form of analytics, advertising, or tracking. We do not collect IP addresses, usage statistics, or device identifiers.

3. OpenAI API Integration

Niceify uses the OpenAI API to provide its functionality. You are required to provide your own API key, which is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us or any third parties.

All communication with OpenAI is handled directly via the API using your key. Data and prompts you send are subject to OpenAI’s privacy policy and terms of service.

4. No Content Handling

Niceify is a utility app that modifies text based on your prompt. The app does not generate, store, or transmit any user-generated or third-party content.

5. Local Use Only

All operations occur locally on your machine, except for API requests made directly to OpenAI using your provided API key.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
